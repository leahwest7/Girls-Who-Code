Gender Equality
More like Gender INEQUALITY in the Workplace

Pay Disparity Statistics

  • In 2020, women make only $0.81 for every dollar a man makes (raw statistic- inclusive of all professions)
  • In controlled gender pay (same job, same qualifications), women make $.98 cents for every dollar a man makes

Reasons for Pay Disparity

  • Family
  • Functionalist perspective- gender inequalities exist to create an efficient division of labor
  • Unconscious bias
  • Conflict perspective- struggle for dominance among social groups (men and women), so men attempt to maintain their power and privalege to the detriment of women
