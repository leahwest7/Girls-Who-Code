Take Action

Want to Change the World? Spread Knowledge, Stifle Ignorance

Women and men are equals. As such, we (women) will demand equality everywhere we go. Encourage other women you know to spread the truth about gender inequality and how it affects women in every country and every profession.

How Can I Support Gender Equality in my Community?

Follow this checklist to get started!

  • Share this website link on social media
  • Organize clubs or community groups advocating for gender equality
  • Do more research about gender inequality beyond just the workplace and inform others of the issue
